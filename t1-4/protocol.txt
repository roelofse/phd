FRAMEWORK: M3 Strategic Decision-Making Under Uncertainty (Roelofse, 2017, 2022)
LOOP NAME: The Gardener's Field — A Regenerative T1–T4 Governance Loop
LOOP TYPE: Closed feedback loop. NOT a linear pipeline.
           T4 Transmitting feeds back to T1 Truthing.
           Each cycle operates at a larger scale (momentum, +L).

---

STAGE DEFINITIONS (present continuous tense — never completed, always in motion)

T1 — TRUTHING [+R, +D]
  Metaphor: Soil Walk + Wilt Detection
  Function: The Sensor — Detection of Emergent Friction
  Question: What is actually happening, and where does it hurt?
  Signal:   Deviation, friction, anomaly density
  Failure:  Insights without decisions. Language increases, action does not.

T2 — TRUSTING [+R, +C]
  Metaphor: Fence Line + Season Check
  Function: The Governor — Boundary Validation + Consent Layer
  Question: What constraints must we trust to remain viable?
            What permissions must be earned before acting?
  Signal:   Constraint compliance, permission state, risk bands
  Failure:  Skipping the fence. Constraints reappear later as failure.
  Note:     T2 is the entry condition for T3. No Transforming without Trusting.

T3 — TRANSFORMING [+S, +C]
  Metaphor: Irrigation + Pruning
  Function: The Processor — Systematic Design
  Question: What design change reduces friction without flattening the system?
  Signal:   Structural improvements, reduced repeated friction
  Failure:  Premature optimization. Structure increases, vitality decreases.
  Note:     Requires explicit permissions from T2. Learning becomes architecture here.

T4 — TRANSMITTING [+S, +D]
  Metaphor: Harvest + Seed-Saving
  Function: The Actuator — Anchor Establishment + Recursion
  Question: What are we delivering, and what are we preserving
            so the next cycle starts smarter?
  Signal:   Deliverables, recursion artifacts (decision record, seed log)
  Failure:  Wins not repeatable. Outcomes celebrated, method not preserved.
  Requirement: Must include a Recursion Log — which T1 signals were addressed,
               which T2 boundaries were respected.
  Note:     T4 output becomes T1's new baseline. This is the loop closing.

---

MOMENTUM (+L)
  Not speed. Phase-appropriate energy.
  Too little: Paralysis — endless T1/T2, never reaching T3. Weeds win by default.
  Too much:  Puppetry — premature T4, no constraint validation. Output up, resilience down.
  Function:  Regulates the phase shift between Responsive half (T1, T2)
             and Systematic half (T3, T4).

---

LOOP FAILURE MODES

Wilderness Drift: Endless sensing without intervention. Momentum too low.
Factory Drift:    Efficient production draining vitality. Momentum too high.

---

MODE MAP (M3 polarized pairs)

  Systematic (+S) ↔ Responsive (+R)
  Conforming (+C) ↔ Differentiating (+D)

  T1: +R, +D  — sense anomalies, treat as signal
  T2: +R, +C  — respect constraints, validate consent
  T3: +S, +C  — design systematically, maintain legibility
  T4: +S, +D  — transmit deliberately, preserve novelty
